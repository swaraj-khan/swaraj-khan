# 💫 About Me:
I build AI systems centered around Large Language Models (LLMs), retrieval architectures, and applied machine learning. My expertise spans the development of **agentic RAG pipelines**, **conversational intelligence tools**, and **ML automation systems**. 

A significant portion of my work focuses on **Voice Agents**, where I specialize in low-latency orchestration and complex conversation flows. I have extensive experience deploying models from **Claude, OpenAI, and Gemini**, as well as managing locally hosted LLMs integrated via custom APIs and CLI workflows. 

My contributions to the field include **5 published IEEE research papers**, a **patented project**, and a technical initiative **funded by the Government of Karnataka**.
